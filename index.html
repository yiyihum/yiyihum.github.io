I am Yiming Huang, a research intern at MSRA, advised by Dr. Weizhu Chen, Dr. Yeyun Gong and Dr. Xiao Liu.

My research focuses on evaluating and enhancing the reasoning capabilities of large language models, particularly in math, code, and agent tasks. I also explore data synthesis and quality assessment.

I am currently looking for PhD positions for Fall 2025.
